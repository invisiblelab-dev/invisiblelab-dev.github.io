Keyruptive
Keyruptive is the most secure and user-friendly mobile application for cryptocurrency.

One of Invisible Lab's first developed projects.

Crypto keys saved in reliable cloud services.

Easy to use mobile application.

Keys stored in separate clouds, adding a layer of security.

One of the few Portuguese patents in the US to date.

We exist to turn the most complex ideas into solutions that are easy to use.

Keyruptive was one of our first developed projects. Our innovative technology creates a digital safe where it is required keys stored in different clouds to open it, adding a new concept of security to Fintech.
The context
Utrust

, the leading cryptocurrency payment solution — see our Utrust case study

here

— invited our CEO Francisco Maia and our CPO Francisco Cruz to be part of their advisory board. This made us study the cryptocurrency ecosystem and its technological foundation - the blockchain.

We wanted to build a product that people can trust and that makes crypto payments safe and easy to manage.

What we did
We transformed cryptographic keys into multiple pieces of data, which individually reveal nothing about the original information. They are stored in independent cloud services.

This way, Keyruptive's mobile application allows users to have access to their funds anytime, anywhere, and gives them the certainty that their keys are safe.

This elegant and effective solution became a US patent and remains to date one of the few software patents made in Portugal.
